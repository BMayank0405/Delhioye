Know Us

Delhi OYE is a nonprofit entity founded in 2014 with the mission todevelop a pool of gender sensitive and responsible individuals to increase awareness, conduct activities and advocate for gender equality. For the same purpose, the organization provides a platform to individuals and organisations to directly work with communities, government officials and corporate sector.The organization envisions to create a society free of gender based discrimination and violence.



Need

In India, gender discrimination and inequality are multifaceted issues that concern men and women alike and, Delhi OYE works to inculcate gender equality in society.Gender parity among the three main stalwarts of society- government, civil community and corporate- promotes prosperity and progressive growth. Gender equality is a human issue and we consider appreciating, respecting and promoting human values.
